About Me:

I am a postdoc at Boston College under the supervision of Spencer Leslie. I finished my PhD at UChicago in 2024 as a student of Ngô Bảo Châu. My thesis studied the geometry of generalized Hitchin systems associated to symmetric varieties. I'm interested broadly in the geometry of generalized Hitchin fibrations and their many manifestations in representation theory and number theory.
